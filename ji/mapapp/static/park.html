VIRUGAMBAKKAM

TAISHA PARK

Taisha Park, located in Virugambakkam, Chennai, is a well-maintained public park situated near the TAISHA (Tamil Nadu All India Service Housing Association) complex, home to many IAS and IPS officers. The park offers various amenities, including a fountain with a light show, rest areas, a tennis court, and a tiled walking path, making it a popular spot for residents of all ages.
